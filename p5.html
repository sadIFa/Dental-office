About us:

With a great love for dentistry, Dr.Vahida Salkić began her private practice in Breza in 2007, after working in public health care for several years before that. Concentrated on her vision of giving remarkable services in dentistry, the private dental practice quickly set off. Professional staff with decades of experience are ready to solve your dental problems and help your smile shine again. The team consists of dr. Vahida Salkić and her dental technicians that have 25 years of experience of dealing with different problems you might have or want to improve on. We will be there for our patients, from the yougest age to older ages. We pride ourselves with caring for our patients and listening to their expectations with great dedication to fulfill their demands. Our central goal is to provide affordable and excellent care for our patients and bring smiles back to their faces.
